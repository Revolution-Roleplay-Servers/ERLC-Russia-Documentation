If you want to use our API features for automation, setting up Discord bots, or managing in-game businesses, you don't need to know how to program at all.

**Our partners already offer ready-made API-based integrations, allowing community members to use them directly.**

<Warning>
  These services are **not created or maintained by the ER:LC Russia team or other project communities**. They are independent third-party products using the API. Be sure to conduct your own due diligence before using them.
</Warning>

## Verified partner services

* **YaMRP Taxi** (https://mrptaxi.site/)
  * Offers: taxi service on the Moscow RolePlay server and convenient management systems for it.

<CardGroup cols={2} />

<Note>
  **We cannot help you choose a specific service and do not provide technical support for the products listed above. For any questions, contact the official support channels of each service.**
</Note>

Before using any third-party service:

* Review the privacy policy and terms of use of that service.
* Make sure the product is actively maintained and has a reliable community.

## Are you a developer?

If you want to create your own integration instead of using ready-made partner solutions, go to the [API Documentation](/en/api-reference/) section to explore all available endpoints.
